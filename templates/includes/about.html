Our Mission

We sell balloons. Except, we don’t just sell balloons. We sell good times. We sell memories. We sell warm fuzzy feelings that stay with you forever.

It’s not just a balloon: it’s celebrating your nan’s 100th birthday or a summer BBQ with friends. It’s the baby shower for the couple who thought they couldn’t conceive and the wedding party for childhood sweethearts. It’s even the Superman costume that made a little boy feel like he could do anything.

We take happiness seriously here. We are with you for every step and celebration, big or small, throughout your journey. The uniqueness of a good party is what keeps us living our values of caring passionately, working collaboratively, thinking innovatively, and most importantly, keeping it fun!

Our Eco Journey

Here at Party Stars, we’re ramping up our efforts to not only become more eco-friendly but to also make our company more sustainable for the long term. We’re changing the way we do things, including how we pack our products, the materials we use in our products and how our products and packaging can be reused or recycled! To make sure we’re getting it right, we’ve partnered with sustainability organisations like Climate Partner to help us as we continue on this journey.
